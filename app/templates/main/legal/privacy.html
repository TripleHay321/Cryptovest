Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected through the platform, how it may be used, how it may be stored, and what rights users may have regarding their personal data.

Privacy Overview
This page covers
  • Personal data collection
  • Account information usage
  • Security and retention
  • Cookies and analytics
  • User rights
  • Third-party sharing

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as name, email address, account credentials, transaction records, support requests, verification documents, device metadata, and usage information when users interact with the platform.

2. How We Use Information

Personal data may be used to create and manage accounts, verify identity, process transactions, provide customer support, improve platform services, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and communicate important operational or compliance-related updates.

3. Legal Basis and Compliance

Depending on your jurisdiction, personal data may be processed based on contractual necessity, legal obligation, legitimate interests, fraud prevention, compliance requirements, or user consent where applicable.

4. Data Sharing

We may share information with service providers, infrastructure partners, email delivery providers, compliance vendors, analytics tools, payment processors, or regulators where required by law or necessary to operate the platform.

5. Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as needed to provide services, satisfy legal and compliance obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, or maintain security and financial records.

6. Security Measures

Reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards should be applied to protect personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or loss. No system, however, can guarantee absolute security.

7. Cookies and Analytics

The platform may use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, measure traffic, improve usability, and analyze product performance. Additional details should be described in the Cookie Policy.

8. International Transfers

If data is processed or stored outside a user’s country of residence, appropriate safeguards and compliance mechanisms may be required depending on applicable law.

9. User Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may have rights to access, correct, update, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of their personal data, as well as rights relating to portability or complaint submission to regulators.

10. Third-Party Services

The platform may integrate with third-party tools such as payment providers, wallet services, analytics vendors, and compliance services. Their handling of information may be governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

11. Children’s Privacy

The platform is not intended for children or individuals below the legally permitted age in the relevant jurisdiction. Accounts should not be created by ineligible users.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the platform after updates may indicate acceptance of the revised policy where permitted by law.
