Risk Disclosure

Important information about market, operational, and financial risk.

Digital assets and investment-related activities involve substantial risk. Users should carefully review and understand these risks before depositing funds, selecting plans, or submitting transactions.

Risk Summary
Key categories
  • Volatility risk
  • Liquidity risk
  • Technology risk
  • Operational risk
  • Regulatory risk
  • Loss of capital

1. No Guaranteed Returns

Any projected return, target outcome, portfolio example, or estimated growth figure shown on the platform is not a guarantee of performance. Users may lose part or all of their capital.

2. Market Volatility

Cryptocurrency and digital asset markets can experience rapid and extreme price fluctuations. Asset values may change significantly over short periods and may be affected by events beyond the platform’s control.

3. Liquidity Risk

Under certain market or operational conditions, it may become difficult to liquidate positions, process withdrawals promptly, or maintain expected pricing and execution conditions.

4. Technology and Infrastructure Risk

Platform operations may be affected by software bugs, system outages, infrastructure failures, wallet service issues, blockchain congestion, API disruptions, cybersecurity incidents, or third-party service interruptions.

5. Operational Risk

Delays, mistakes, fraud attempts, human error, administrative review processes, or external provider dependency may affect deposits, withdrawals, account verification, and transaction processing.

6. Regulatory and Legal Risk

Digital asset regulation continues to evolve. Changes in law, tax treatment, licensing requirements, sanctions regimes, or enforcement activity may affect platform availability, account access, transaction eligibility, or service structure.

7. Counterparty and Custody Risk

Where third-party wallet providers, exchanges, custodians, payment processors, or infrastructure vendors are involved, users may be exposed to additional counterparty and operational risks outside direct platform control.

8. User Error Risk

Incorrect wallet addresses, wrong network selection, failed security practices, phishing, password misuse, or device compromise may result in account loss, delayed transactions, or irreversible loss of funds.

9. Past or Projected Performance

Historical examples, model projections, sample plan outcomes, or interface illustrations should not be relied upon as indicators of future results.

10. Independent Judgment

Users are responsible for assessing whether platform services, investment options, and digital asset activity are appropriate for their risk tolerance, financial position, and legal circumstances.

11. Seek Professional Advice

Users should consider obtaining legal, financial, accounting, and tax advice before making decisions involving digital assets or investment-related activity.

12. Final Acknowledgment

By using the platform, users acknowledge that they understand digital asset activity involves risk and that losses, delays, and adverse outcomes may occur.
